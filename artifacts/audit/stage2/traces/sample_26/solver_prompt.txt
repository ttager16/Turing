- Reason: "Cyclic transfer pattern detected"

**6. High Frequency Account Activity:**

- Reason: "High frequency account activity"

**7. Nested Money Laundering:**

- Flag consecutive transaction pairs
- Reason: "Rapid sequential transactions"
