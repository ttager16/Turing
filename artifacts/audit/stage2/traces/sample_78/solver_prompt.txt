### Problem Statement

The trading firm continuously receives updates from several exchanges, where each update includes a stock’s symbol, volatility, and volume. The goal is to consolidate this information into a single global ranking of stocks, ordered by their combined priority score derived from volatility and volume.

Your task is to implement an algorithm that:
